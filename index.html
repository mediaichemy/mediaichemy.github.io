Manifesto

The pressure to create content has become overwhelming. Feeling like you need to become an influencer just to get by is now the norm. Whether you're an artist, comedian, teacher, dentist, construction worker, or psychologist, everyone is expected to produce content—just to be seen, to survive, to earn a decent living.

Meanwhile, AI is creating art, music, images, and videos, while most of us struggle just to get by. If the algorithmic rules of social media demand we flood it with endless content, this is the tool to do it.

We use the most cost-effective AI solutions to make this accessible to as many people as possible. As the library evolves, we will continue to add more AI providers, prioritizing capability and affordability.
